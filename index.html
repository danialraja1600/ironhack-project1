Never Give Up

By Danial Raja

Instructions

Catch the blue square to gain points.
You begin the game with three lives
If you recieve ten points, you will recieve an extra life
Use the arrows keys, left and right to move your player.
You must avoid the red square. Collision with the red square will cause you to lose a life.
Use the spacebar to shoot the red square.
Killing a red square will allow you to obtain one life.
